Diary Dawn

Diary Dawn is a comprehensive journaling platform designed to help users track their thoughts, feelings, and experiences. Featuring various diary entry types such as gratitude, affirmation, and dream journals, Diary Dawn leverages machine learning to provide insightful superpower analyses and personalized feedback. Explore the many tools and features aimed at enhancing your mental well-being and self-discovery.

Cortex Connections

Cortex Connections is a neuroscience project focused on exploring the differences in brain connectivity between individuals with ADHD and neurotypical individuals. Utilizing advanced neuroimaging techniques and machine learning algorithms, this project aims to visualize and analyze brain activity to uncover unique patterns and insights. Dive into the fascinating world of brain connectivity and discover the intricacies of the human mind.
